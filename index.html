What we do

#DataDawgs is concerned with topics in Machine Learning, Informatics, and Data Science.

Coding Workshops

#DataDawgs will be hosting workshops in Python programming, which has become one of the most important tools for data scinetists in today's world.

Networking

#DataDawgs will be bringing in professors and industry professionals to lead presentations on how to be succseful as a data scientists.

Developing Data Science Skills

#DataDawgs will be hosting workshops on different techniques and practices that every aspiring data scientists should know.

Data Science in Action

In the future, we hope to participate in Kaggle data science competitions as a team.
